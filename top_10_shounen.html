Top 10 Shounen Anime

10.Jujutsu Kaisen

The hit shonen action series Jujutsu Kaisen, based on the manga of the same name, is really making waves in the anime community.

Jujutsu Kaisen was releaed on November 25, 2019. The anime came with the excellent animation and and exiting storyline.

It’s a light hearted shounen anime. While watching this you might feel like the liter version on demon slayer. But the universe of jujutsu kaisen will be grow in future.

Jujutsu Kaisen having 24 episodes in the 1st season.

9.Naruto

Naruto is the one of the most famous shounen anime in entire anime history.

Naruto is the best example to describe shounen anime with excellent storyline. The story and soundtrack will blow your mind. If you ask me then, I would recommend you Naruto first.

Naruto is divided into two anime series Naruto (220 episodes) and Naruto: Shippuden (500 episodes).

If you start watching this anime then I am can definitely say that you will be addicted to this. You connect to Naruto emotionally

The only weakness of Naruto is it’s filled with fillers.

8.Kimetsu no Yaiba (Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba)

Not any anime can compare the animation to the Demon Slayer. Demon Slayer will surpass all your animation expectations.

The main strength of this anime is it’s fight scenes. The fight scenes are well choreographed, the have put right element at the right places, emotions, techniques, surprises all the these things are in point.

Ufotable, Inc made the Demon Slayer with outstanding animation.

Demon Slayer didn’t change the shounen genre. But it was a masterclass in how a traditional shounen anime should look like.

7.My Hero Academia (Boku no Hero Academia)

When the “legendary” shounen series finally end, you don’t have to lose hope for the genre: Boku no Hero Academia is proudly carrying its flag for the new generation.

My Hero Academia comes under modern shounen anime. It’s never lacking in its funny and it has valuable lessons for kids and kids at heart, especially on themes of being a good person, of being a hero in your own little way.

My Hero might not be doing anything wholly new, but author Kohei Horikoshi is taking the tropes of the genre and executing them better than anyone else in the game right now. The only thing holding the series back is it is simply too new.

6.Gintama

Well now let’s move on funny shounen anime.

What if I told you there is a anime which mocking every other anime but still rules. Yes! I am talking about Gintama.

Gintama is a very funny shounen anime which makes parody of other animes. It isn’t as much about the story as much as it’s about the characters and their hilarious antics.

Gintama is one of the longest running anime series ever.

5.Attack on Titan (Shingeki no Kyojin)

If you want full of thriller, action, adventure, this should be on top of your watchlist.

When you will watch Attack on Titan you will feel something that you won’t ever feel before.You will feel Grand.

The moment you watch this audiovisual masterpiece you will immediately appreciate the level of thought and detail that went into this.

In short this anime is simply amazing! The way it is animated, the soundtrack, the characters, the story, the thrill and the suspense.It is a all in one action-packed anime.

4.JoJo’s Bizarre Adventure (JoJo no Kimyou na Bouken)

If the criteria for the best anime ever was the number of memes it spawned, JoJo’s Bizarre Adventure would at least land at the top five.

Ever since its debut in 2013, JoJo’s Bizarre Adventure has slowly but surely gained popularity, especially among Western anime fans.

While watching JoJo’s Bizarre Adventure you will never get bored. This series having it's own unique art style. Going forward, the series becomes more and more interesting.

3. Hunter x Hunter

The first adaptation of Hunter x Hunter was in 1999 (92 episodes) and the second adaptation was in 2011 (148 episodes).

Hunter x Hunter is like Harry Potter, explores more serious and darker themes as time goes by.

Shounen but not typical one. Hunter x Hunter is not having extra characters, it gives the equal amount of screen time to every charater. Hunter x Hunter does not lose its potential in entire series. At last you will astonished where the series started and where it ended.

In short the anime is simply epic.

2. One Piece

Alongside Naruto and Bleach, One Piece is one of the three big shounen series of anime.

Sure, fans are seeing signs that the manga is nearing the final arc. But no one really knows how.

Eiichiro Oda could continue his story for a decade more because that’s just how entertaining One Piece is.

The anime started in 1999 and it’s still ongoing after 900 episodes.

One Piece inherits all qualities like Shounen, Action, Adventure and Emotion. In short the anime is all rounder.

1. Fullmetal Alchemist : Brotherhood

It’s a bit amusing seeing the similarities between Hunter x Hunter (2011) and FMA: Brotherhood.

Both series are the second anime adaptations of the manga source material, and most fans prefer them over the first adaptations.

The first adaptation Fullmetal Alchemist was in 2003 (51 episodes) and the second adaptation Fullmetal Alchemist : Brotherhood was in 2003 (64 episodes).

It’s been 12 years, and it’s still the No. 1 anime overall on My Anime List.

Edward Elric and Alphonse Elric are top-tier main characters. And the same goes for its many supporting characters like Roy Mustang, Riza Hawkeye, and Winry Rockbell.
